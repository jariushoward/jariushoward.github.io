Overview

My name is Jarius Howard. I was born on July 24, 2001 in Portsmouth, VA. I am the second child of Ardonia and Eldridge Howard. I have one sibling, a brother, named PJ Howard. We are eight years apart.

I grew up watching football. Since I was born, I would go to my older brother's games. Being around the game since a young age, I always knew that one day I wanted to play football. After months of begging my dad to let me play, he finally signed me up to play flag football for the Norfolk Saints, a pop warner organization.

When I went to my first football practice in August 2005 I was only five years old. At the conclusion of my first practice, my coaches asked my dad if they could move me up to Tiny-Mite tackle football because I was too advanced for flag. My dad gave them the okay and I officially became the youngest player on my team.

I played for the Norfolk Saints from kindergarten to seventh grade. In eighth grade, instead of playing for the Norfolk Saints, I played for the Pop Warner organization, the Virginia Beach Mustangs. We finished the season fifth in the nation.

At the conclusion of my last Pop Warner season, it was time for me to prepare for my high school football career.
