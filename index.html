Overview

My name is Jarius Howard. I was born on July 24, 2001. I was born and raised in Portsmouth, VA. I am the second child of Ardonia and Eldridge Howard. I have one sibling, a brother, named PJ Howard. We are eight years apart.

I grew up watching football on TV and in person. Since I was born, I would go to my older brother's football games. Being around the game so much, I always knew I wanted to play. I told my dad that I wanted to play and he first signed me up when I was five-years-old. He signed me up to play flag football for the Pop Warner organization, the Norfolk Saints.

When I went to my first football practice in August 2005, the coaches said that I was too advanced for flag football and they moved me up to Tiny-Mite. I was the youngest on my team and I was playing tackle football.

I played for the Norfolk Saints from kindergarten to seventh grade. In eighth grade, instead of playing for the Norfolk Saints, I played for the Pop Warner organization, the Virginia Beach Mustangs. We finished the season fifth in the nation.

At the conclusion of my last Pop Warner season, it was time for me to prepare for my high school career.
