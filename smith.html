Oscar Smith

I played football all four years of high school, but my career didn't begin to take off until my junior season. After playing RB my entire life, my junior year, I decided to make the switch to WR.

I earned the Most Outstanding WR Award, which was the WR MVP Award for my team.

I earned All-District and All-Region WR honors making me one of the top players in the area at my position.

By my senior year, I was ranked as the third best WR in the state of VA for the class of 2019.
