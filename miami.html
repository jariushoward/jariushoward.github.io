University of Miami

After graduating from high school in June 2019, two weeks later it was time for me to report to the University of Miami to begin my college football career. I redshirted my freshman year to focus on gaining weight and adjusting to the speed of the collegiate level.

In the spring of my freshman year, March 2020, the entire team got sent home because of Covid-19 after only having three spring football practices.

When we returned to school to prepare for the season, the first day of official practice, August 7, 2020, I suffered a career ending injury. I tore my ACL, PCL, LCL, and PLC in my right knee. I also suffered nerve damage in my right foot.

I ended up having to have two separate surgeries. One to repair my knee and the other to repair the nerve in my foot.

My college football career was over before it even began. My football career may be over, but my life is just beginning. I graduate from the University of Miami on December 16, 2022.
