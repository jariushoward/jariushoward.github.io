University of Miami

I enrolled at the University of Miami to begin my college football career July 2019. I redshirted my freshman season to focus on gaining weight and preparing my body for the collegiate level.

In the spring of freshman year, preparing for my sophomore season, we all got sent home because of Covid-19.

When we returned to school to prepare for the season, the first day of practice I suffered a career ending injury. I tore my ACL, PCL, LCL, and suffered nerve damage in my foot.

I ended up having two surgeries, one to repair my knee and the other to fix the nerve in my foot. I had my surgeries two years apart.
